/ Frequently Asked Questions

  1. What are ExaQuo Journals?

     +

    ExaQuo Journals is a journal foundation under the TEVx organization that provides a platform for publishing research articles, academic papers, and thought leadership content in various fields. It aims to promote academic and scientific research, innovation, and development through the dissemination of high-quality research publications.

  2. What kind of journals does ExaQuo publish??

    +

    ExaQuo publishes a range of academic journals in different fields such as science, technology, engineering, social sciences, and humanities. Each journal has a specific scope and aims to cover the latest research and developments in its respective field. Some of the journals published by ExaQuo include the Journal of Engineering and Technology, Journal of Social Sciences and Humanities, and Journal of Science and Innovation.

  3. How does the peer-review process work at ExaQuo Journals?

    +

    ExaQuo Journals follows a rigorous peer-review process to ensure the quality and validity of published articles. Once a manuscript is submitted, it is first reviewed by the editorial team to check for adherence to the journal's guidelines and suitability for publication. The manuscript is then sent to two or more independent reviewers with expertise in the relevant field. The reviewers provide detailed feedback and suggestions for improvement, which the authors must address before the article can be accepted for publication.

  4. How can I submit my manuscript to ExaQuo Journals?

    +

    Authors can submit their manuscripts to ExaQuo Journals through the online submission system on the ExaQuo website. They need to register an account and follow the submission guidelines, including formatting, word count, and referencing style. Once submitted, the manuscript will be assigned to an editor who will oversee the peer-review process.
